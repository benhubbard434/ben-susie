                            Transport & Hotels                            

Getting there

Driving

You can easily drive to both Royston church, and The Manor Barn - the closest main roads are the M11 and the A10.

Parking is available around the church, and on site and you can leave your car at the barn overnight. Cars need to be collected by 11am the following morning.

Google Maps

Public transport

Nearest station: Royston followed by a 20 minute walk, or Taxi to the church

  • Panther Taxis | |

Getting home

Last train

The last nearby train to London is from Royston, at 9 minutes past midnight. To make sure you catch this, we recommend booking a Taxi in advance.

Hotels

If you'd like to book a hotel, we recommend heading to Cambridge as there is a wider choice than nearby Royston.
We'd advise booking any taxis in advance.

Suggested Hotels

  • IBIS Cambridge Central Station

  • Travelodge Cambridge Central

  • University Arms Hotel

These hotels are based on the same side of Cambridge as the Barn, but there are many more hotels and Airbnb's available in Cambridge and the surrounding area for all different budgets. Please let us know if you have any questions or need help finding where to stay!!
