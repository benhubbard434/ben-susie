                            Transport & Hotels                            

Getting there

Driving

You can easily drive to The Manor Barn - the closest main roads are the M11 and the A10.

Parking is available on site and you can leave your car there overnight. Cars need to be collected by 11am the following morning.

Google Maps

Public transport

Nearest station: Cambridge followed by a Taxi to the venue

If you're coming from London, the barn is situated between Royston and Cambridge train stations - it's just under an hour on the train. We'd recommend heading to Cambridge as there will be more taxi's available.

Getting home

Last train

The last nearby train to London is from Royston, at 9 minutes past midnight. To make sure you catch this, we recommend booking a Taxi in advance.

Hotels

If you'd like to book a hotel, we recommend heading to Cambridge as there is a wider choice than nearby Royston.
We'd advise booking any taxis in advance.
